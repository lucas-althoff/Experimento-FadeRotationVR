Fade-rotation: Attention-driving Mechanism for Content-adaptive Cinematic VR

Subjective Experiment to Understand the Effects of Fade Rotation.

Cinematic Virtual Reality

360-degree videos

36 Clips

(30s each)

40 minutes

 

Immersive experiences conducted by cinematic VR (CVR) videos are being delivered to viewers in an unprecedented pace, reaching massive numbers of content produced by amateurs and professionals. The aim of this study is to propose an attention-driving mechanism called fade rotation and find acceptable parameters to apply it accordingly to content conditions. You will contribute with the research of University of Brasília in the field of Immerssive Media, 360° videos, please check our safety protocols and Schedule a session with our team.

Fade-rotation: Attention-driving Mechanism for Content-adaptive Cinematic VR
Lucas Althoff*, Mylène C. Q. Farias*, ACM International Conference on Interactive Media Experiences (IMX 2022).
